Monsters

General Info

As the name of the series implies, Monster Hunter is a series where the player is tasked with hunting various monsters. There are over 200 monsters including subspecies, variants, and deviants. Each monster has a distinctive feature that allows it to stand out from others. Some of these monsters can range from an large bear, Arzuros, to a legendary black dragon, Fatalis, that was said to have wiped out an entire county in one night. Monsters tend to be territorial, and recent games allow them to shake things up by duking it out with another monster during a hunter's quest. Every monster has a weakness to different elements, and certain weapons work better on different parts of the monsters. Researching these creatures and finding out what monster is weakest to what attack is key to completing your quest.

Weapons

General Info

In order to defend themselves out in the field, hunters equip one of multiple different weapons. These weapons can range from the classic great sword, to the unusual hunting horn. Every weapons has a unique playstyle and damage type that works better against different monsters and various monster parts. Bladed weapons, like the dual blades and great sword, do slash damage. Blunt weapons, like the hammer and the hunting horn, inflict blunt damage. Ranged weapons, like the bow and light bowgun, inflict ranged damage. Weapons also come with an element or ailment, effects that can inflict various status effects onto a monster. With over a dozen choices in recent games, hunters should have no problem picking out a favorite.

Companions

General Info

While hunters are strong warriors, they don't work alone. Ever since the original Moster Hunter, hunters have been working alongside helpful felynes. Felynes are cat helpers who assist the hunters in various ways, whether it be as a chef who prepares hefty meals, a housekeeper, or as a palico assisting the hunters in battle with various weapons and gadgets. Monster Hunter Rise expands on the companions wiht the introduction of palamutes. Palamutes are canine companions that can help fight monster like palicos, and they can let their hunter mount them to ride around the various locals at top speeds. Monster Hunter Rise: Sunbreak goes one step further, allowing fellow villagers to join the hunter out in the field. Each villager has their own fighting style, selection of weapons, and unique interactions with each other out in the field.
